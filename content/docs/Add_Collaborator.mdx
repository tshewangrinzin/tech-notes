In this guide, you'll learn how to invite other developers to collaborate on your GitHub project. Adding collaborators gives them direct push access to your repository, allowing for efficient team development.

<Callout title="Team Collaboration">
Directly adding collaborators is ideal for small teams or private projects where you want specific individuals to have write access without going through the fork-and-pull-request workflow.
</Callout>

---

## Inviting a Collaborator

Follow these steps to add a new member to your GitHub repository.

<Steps>
<Step title="Access Repository Settings">
Navigate to the main page of your repository on GitHub and click on the **Settings** tab located in the top navigation bar.

![Access Settings](https://images.tango.us/workflows/5765305f-1004-4164-9b23-faf179844fe0/steps/7054e720-1c2a-4e60-bc53-3b962fccd5e2/1245ba38-261c-46d6-8e1a-f6043f1bd963.png?mark-x=1452&mark-y=112&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz0xNjAmaD04MiZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
</Step>

<Step title="Navigate to Collaborators">
In the left sidebar menu, find the **Access** section and click on **Collaborators**.

![Navigate to Collaborators](https://images.tango.us/workflows/5765305f-1004-4164-9b23-faf179844fe0/steps/de4be14d-77ea-454c-8a17-39d5738d6d99/16d93715-ff85-420c-8927-ddc4e31407e5.png?mark-x=56&mark-y=402&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz02MDgmaD04MiZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
</Step>

<Step title="Authenticate Access">
For security reasons, GitHub may ask you to confirm your identity. Click on **Use passkey** or follow the prompts to verify your access to the settings.

![Authenticate Access](https://images.tango.us/workflows/5765305f-1004-4164-9b23-faf179844fe0/steps/4df70e7e-0055-4a59-a61e-74bf0c5176f5/7ae3f067-dc69-4a36-8210-d170c0cb113a.png?mark-x=804&mark-y=582&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01MjQmaD04MCZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
</Step>

<Step title="Click Add People">
On the Collaborators page, click the **Add people** button to open the invitation dialog.

![Click Add People](https://images.tango.us/workflows/5765305f-1004-4164-9b23-faf179844fe0/steps/7a688890-fc02-477f-8ade-f9e3859133c3/b3607b57-65cb-418b-98f1-6a16d4bc59c1.png?mark-x=1858&mark-y=754&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz0yMTgmaD04MCZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
</Step>

<Step title="Search for Collaborator">
Type the GitHub username or email address (e.g., `tshewang.rinzin2016@gmail.com`) of the person you want to invite.

![Search for Collaborator](https://images.tango.us/workflows/5765305f-1004-4164-9b23-faf179844fe0/steps/66fa0ce9-5297-4e36-b12f-09fccd5a6757/81cf4f07-2e23-4715-b27f-2f7c66e23000.png?mark-x=450&mark-y=790&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz0xMjMyJmg9ODAmZml0PWNyb3AmY29ybmVyLXJhZGl1cz0xMA%3D%3D)
</Step>

<Step title="Select User Result">
Click on the user record that appears in the dropdown to select them.

![Select User](https://images.tango.us/workflows/5765305f-1004-4164-9b23-faf179844fe0/steps/d1ac5d8b-6417-4c98-9316-f3f96daa7283/0949ba17-ff40-4b6e-809e-6c78a12bc019.png?mark-x=468&mark-y=904&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz0xMTk2Jmg9MTEyJmZpdD1jcm9wJmNvcm5lci1yYWRpdXM9MTA%3D)
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm Invitation">
Click the **Add [username] to this repository** button to send the invitation.

![Confirm Invitation](https://images.tango.us/workflows/5765305f-1004-4164-9b23-faf179844fe0/steps/5c0d76fc-fdbd-4965-8fdf-41a9a588dff4/708f1f15-023e-4e45-aa82-46ff5d8cc3c3.png?mark-x=1102&mark-y=966&m64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL2JsYW5rLnBuZz9tYXNrPWNvcm5lcnMmYm9yZGVyPTQlMkNGRjc0NDImdz01NjQmaD04MCZmaXQ9Y3JvcCZjb3JuZXItcmFkaXVzPTEw)
</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Invitation Sent!">
The user will receive an email invitation and a notification on GitHub. They must accept the invitation before they gain access to the repository.
</Callout>
